## Monitoring Market Opportunity Calculation

### Assumptions
- Total APAC infrastructure value: $1.42 trillion (2025)
- Critical infrastructure requiring monitoring: ~30% = $426 billion
- Monitoring system cost: 0.5-2% of asset value
- **Addressable monitoring market:** $2.1 - $8.5 billion

### SHM/GTIM Market Sizing Validation
- SHM APAC market: $1.2-3.1B (from earlier research)
- GTIM global market: $5.7B (2024)
- GTIM APAC (35-40%): $2.0-2.3B
- **Combined SHM+GTIM APAC:** ~$3-5B (consistent with calculation)
