## Pricing Intelligence (Preliminary)

**Typical Project Sizes:**
- Small monitoring project: $50K - $200K
- Medium project: $200K - $1M
- Large project: $1M - $5M+
- Enterprise deployment: $5M - $20M+

**Pricing Models:**
- Hardware: $500 - $5,000 per sensor (wide range)
- Software: $10K - $100K annual subscription
- Services: $150 - $300/hour consulting
- Turnkey: 15-30% margin on total project

**Note:** Detailed pricing requires deeper competitive analysis and RFP data.
