## Key Market Drivers

1. **Infrastructure Development**
   - Rapid urbanization in APAC
   - Aging infrastructure in developed markets
   - Government infrastructure spending

2. **Regulatory Mandates**
   - GISTM for mining tailings
   - ISO standards for structural safety
   - Insurance requirements

3. **Technology Trends**
   - Wireless SHM adoption (37.6% of new installations in APAC)
   - IoT integration
   - AI/ML analytics
   - Cloud-based platforms

4. **Risk Mitigation**
   - Recent infrastructure failures
   - Climate change impacts
   - Seismic activity in APAC
