## 1. CORE FRAMEWORKS

### Content Marketing Funnel (TOFU, MOFU, BOFU)

**TOFU (Top of Funnel) - Awareness:**
- **Goal:** Attract broad audience, build awareness
- **Content Types:** Blog posts, social media, educational videos, infographics
- **Metrics:** Traffic, reach, impressions
- **For Intelltech:** "What is SHMS?", "Geotechnical monitoring basics", "Mining safety trends"

**MOFU (Middle of Funnel) - Consideration:**
- **Goal:** Engage and educate prospects
- **Content Types:** Webinars, case studies, detailed guides, comparison content
- **Metrics:** Engagement, time on page, downloads
- **For Intelltech:** "SHMS vs Manual Monitoring", "ROI Calculator", "Implementation Guide"

**BOFU (Bottom of Funnel) - Decision:**
- **Goal:** Drive conversions and sales
- **Content Types:** Product demos, free trials, testimonials, pricing guides
- **Metrics:** Conversions, MQLs, SQLs
- **For Intelltech:** "Request Demo", "Customer Success Stories", "Pricing & Packages"

### Hero, Hub, Hygiene Content Model

**Hero Content:**
- **Frequency:** 1-2x per year
- **Scale:** Large, high-impact campaigns
- **Examples:** Product launch, industry report, major event
- **For Intelltech:** Annual "State of Mining Safety" report, major product launch

**Hub Content:**
- **Frequency:** Weekly or bi-weekly
- **Scale:** Regular, consistent engagement
- **Examples:** Blog series, newsletters, podcasts
- **For Intelltech:** Weekly "SHMS Insights" blog, bi-weekly newsletter

**Hygiene Content:**
- **Frequency:** Always-on
- **Scale:** Evergreen, SEO-driven
- **Examples:** FAQs, how-to guides, glossaries
- **For Intelltech:** "SHMS Buyer's Guide", "Geotechnical Terms Glossary", "Installation FAQ"

### Pillar Content Strategy

**Concept:** Create comprehensive "pillar" pages that cover broad topics, then build "cluster" content that links back.

**Example for Intelltech:**

**Pillar:** "Complete Guide to SHMS in Mining"
- **Cluster 1:** Types of sensors used in SHMS
- **Cluster 2:** Data analysis techniques
- **Cluster 3:** Regulatory compliance
- **Cluster 4:** Case studies by mine type
- **Cluster 5:** Integration with existing systems

**Benefits:**
- Improved SEO (internal linking)
- Establishes authority
- Better user experience

## 2. KEY STRATEGIES

### SEO-Driven Content

**Process:**
1. **Keyword Research:**
   - Use tools: Ahrefs, SEMrush, Google Keyword Planner
   - For Intelltech: "SHMS mining", "geotechnical monitoring", "tailings dam safety"
   
2. **Content Creation:**
   - Incorporate keywords naturally (2-3% density)
   - Use keywords in title, H1, H2, meta description
   - Write for humans first, SEO second
   
3. **On-Page SEO:**
   - Meta tags (title, description)
   - Alt text for images
   - Internal linking
   - Mobile-friendly design
   
4. **Off-Page SEO:**
   - Build backlinks from industry sites
   - Guest posting
   - PR and media mentions

**Expected Results:** 50-100% increase in organic traffic within 6 months

### Thought Leadership

**Goal:** Position Intelltech as THE authority in SHMS and geotechnical monitoring

**Tactics:**
- **Original Research:** Conduct surveys, publish findings
- **Industry Commentary:** React to news, regulations, incidents
- **Expert Opinions:** Share predictions, best practices
- **Speaking:** Conferences, webinars, podcasts

**Content Examples:**
- "The Future of Tailings Dam Monitoring: 5 Predictions for 2027"
- "Why 80% of Mining Companies Will Adopt SHMS by 2030"
- "Lessons from [Recent Mining Incident]: How SHMS Could Have Prevented It"

### Case Studies & White Papers

**Case Study Structure:**
1. **Challenge:** What problem did the client face?
2. **Solution:** How did Intelltech's SHMS solve it?
3. **Results:** Quantifiable outcomes (cost savings, safety improvements)
4. **Testimonial:** Quote from client

**Example:**
- **Title:** "How [Mining Company] Reduced Geotechnical Incidents by 75% with SHMS"
- **Challenge:** Manual monitoring was slow, error-prone, costly
- **Solution:** Deployed Intelltech SHMS with 200 sensors
- **Results:** 75% fewer incidents, $2M saved annually, 90% faster data collection
- **Testimonial:** "Intelltech's SHMS transformed our safety operations" - CTO

**White Paper Structure:**
1. **Executive Summary:** Key findings in 1 page
2. **Problem Statement:** Industry challenge
3. **Analysis:** Data, research, expert insights
4. **Solution:** How SHMS addresses the problem
5. **Conclusion:** Call to action

**Example:**
- **Title:** "The Hidden Costs of Manual Geotechnical Monitoring: A $10B Industry Problem"

### Video & Multimedia Content

**Types:**
- **Explainer Videos:** "What is SHMS?" (2-3 min)
- **Product Demos:** "SHMS Dashboard Walkthrough" (5-7 min)
- **Customer Testimonials:** "Why [Company] Chose Intelltech" (1-2 min)
- **Webinars:** "Best Practices in Tailings Dam Monitoring" (45-60 min)

**Distribution:**
- YouTube (SEO benefits)
- LinkedIn (B2B audience)
- Website (landing pages)
- Email campaigns

### Content Repurposing

**Example: One White Paper → 10+ Assets**

1. **Original:** 20-page white paper on SHMS ROI
2. **Blog Series:** 5 blog posts, each covering one chapter
3. **Infographic:** Visual summary of key stats
4. **LinkedIn Posts:** 10 posts with key insights
5. **Webinar:** 45-min presentation based on findings
6. **Email Series:** 5-email drip campaign
7. **Case Study:** Detailed example from white paper
8. **Video:** 5-min animated summary
9. **Podcast Episode:** Interview with author
10. **Slide Deck:** SlideShare presentation

**Benefit:** 10x content output from 1x effort

## 4. IMPLEMENTATION ROADMAP FOR MOTHER

### Phase 1: Automated Blog Post Generation (Weeks 1-4)

**Objective:** Generate 2-3 SEO-optimized blog posts per week

**Steps:**
1. **Topic Research:**
   - Use Ahrefs to identify high-value keywords
   - Analyze competitor content
   - Identify content gaps
   
2. **Content Generation:**
   - Use MOTHER + OpenAI to draft posts (1500-2000 words)
   - Include: intro, 3-5 main sections, conclusion, CTA
   - Optimize for target keyword
   
3. **Quality Control:**
   - Human review for technical accuracy
   - Add images, charts, examples
   - Ensure brand voice consistency
   
4. **Publishing & Distribution:**
   - Publish to website
   - Share on LinkedIn
   - Include in newsletter

**Expected Output:** 8-12 blog posts per month

### Phase 2: Case Study Creation (Weeks 5-8)

**Objective:** Create 2-3 compelling case studies

**Steps:**
1. **Data Collection:**
   - Interview clients
   - Gather quantitative results
   - Collect testimonials
   
2. **Content Creation:**
   - Use MOTHER to draft case study (1000-1500 words)
   - Follow structure: Challenge → Solution → Results
   - Include visuals (charts, photos)
   
3. **Design:**
   - Create PDF version with professional design
   - Add client logo (with permission)
   
4. **Distribution:**
   - Landing page on website
   - Gated download (lead generation)
   - Share on LinkedIn
   - Use in sales presentations

**Expected Output:** 2-3 case studies per month

### Phase 3: White Paper & Technical Content (Weeks 9-16)

**Objective:** Produce 1 comprehensive white paper per quarter

**Steps:**
1. **Research:**
   - Conduct industry survey (optional)
   - Analyze data, trends
   - Interview experts
   
2. **Content Creation:**
   - Use MOTHER to draft 15-20 page white paper
   - Include: executive summary, problem, analysis, solution, conclusion
   - Add charts, graphs, citations
   
3. **Review & Refinement:**
   - Technical review by engineers
   - Fact-checking
   - Professional editing
   
4. **Design & Publishing:**
   - Professional PDF design
   - Landing page with gated download
   - Promotional campaign (email, LinkedIn, ads)

**Expected Output:** 1 white paper per quarter (4 per year)

### Phase 4: Multi-Channel Distribution (Ongoing)

**Objective:** Maximize reach and engagement across all channels

**Tactics:**
1. **Content Repurposing:**
   - Turn white paper into 5 blog posts
   - Create infographic from key stats
   - Produce video summary
   
2. **Social Media:**
   - Daily LinkedIn posts (mix of original + curated)
   - Weekly Twitter threads
   - Monthly LinkedIn articles
   
3. **Email Marketing:**
   - Bi-weekly newsletter
   - Drip campaigns for new leads
   - Re-engagement campaigns
   
4. **Paid Promotion:**
   - LinkedIn sponsored posts for top content
   - Google Ads for high-intent keywords
   - Retargeting campaigns

**Expected Results:**
- 3x increase in content reach
- 2x increase in lead generation
- 50% increase in content-attributed revenue
